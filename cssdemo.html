Cricket

It is an outdoor game which requires a bat, ball and stumps. It is usually played on a field by two teams and the team that scores more runs, wins the game. Umpires are also present to give a fair decision and stop arguments. Cricket is the national game of England.

IPL

Indian Premier League was the one of its kind ever held in India. It is an Indian League of Cricket with 20/20 cricket matches between different states of India. However each team consisted of players from different countries. There were a number of famous entrepreneurs who started 10 teams. Names of all the remarkable players were put in the list to be put in the auction and just like an auction functions, they were bid for and the highest bidder had him in his/her team.
